### Biography
Valerian Hall-Chen is an adjunct assistant professor at School of Mathematical and Physical Sciences, Nanyang Technological University. Valerian is also Principal Investigator and Head of the Plasma Physics & Diagnostics at the Future Energy Acceleration and Translation Centre, A\*STAR. Valerian holds a BA in Natural Sciences (Physics) from the University of Cambridge and a DPhil in Theoretical Physics from the University of Oxford, where he was supervised by Felix Parra and Jon Hillesheim.
### Research Interests
Microwave diagnostics, synthetic diagnostics, plasma exhaust, edge and sheath physics, and heating and current drive.
