### Research Interests 

During his Ph.D. studies at Dalian University of Technology, Jiayang has been working on:
- The interaction between tungsten fuzz and plasma in fusion devices, including its growth, erosion, heat transfer, and deuterium retention, using the SURO-FUZZ code developed by the team.
- Heat transfer and multiphase flow in porous media based on the Lattice Boltzmann Method (LBM), implemented with a self-developed LBS code.

At NTU, his research will focus on:

- The impact of magnetic ripple on impurity transport in fusion devices, using the GYSELA code.
