### Biography 

Haluk Sait Aksoy is enrolled in PhD studies at the School of Physical and Mathematical Sciences at Nanyang Technological University (NTU) under the supervision of Kyungtak Lim, starting in January 2026. He obtained a Bachelor of Science in Nuclear Engineering from UNIST, South Korea, and a Master of Science in Nuclear Fusion and Engineering Physics through the Erasmus Mundus FUSION-EP program in Europe.
